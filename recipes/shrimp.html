Best shrimp ever

Description

Think shrimp scampi meets a crunchy baked Parmesan topping. You can serve them as an appetizer, on top of cooked pasta with extra lemon-butter sauce, or simply with some grilled bread and flavorful olive oil to dip in.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Olive oil
  • Lemom
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • Spices
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F
  3. Peel shrimp, leaving tails intact
  4. Use a paring knife to cut lengthwise down vein side of shrimp, cutting almost but not all the way through the shrimp
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until shrimp are cooked through, 8 to 10 minutes
