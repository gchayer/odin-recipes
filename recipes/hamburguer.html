Best hamburger ever

Description

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling! This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Soy sauce
  • Spices
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat
  2. Lightly oil the grate
  3. Combine ingredients in bowl
  4. Form 4 patties.
  5. Cook patties 4 to 5 minutes
